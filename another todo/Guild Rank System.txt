# 🛡️ **Eldoria Adventurer Guild Rank System**

*“Where merit is carved not by bloodline, but by the monsters one leaves behind.”*

In Eldoria, Guild Ranks are earned through **merit, quests, and monster slaying records**. With each rank, the guild grants higher-risk assignments, access to restricted zones, and increasing rewards.

---

# 🏅 **Guild Rank List (Beginner → Legendary)**

## **F — Initiate**

*“A wanderer with potential, yet untested by fang or shadow.”*
**Requirements:** None (entry rank when joining the Guild)
**Privileges:**

* Access to basic quests
* Allowed in safe training zones
* No dangerous contracts

---

## **E — Novice**

*“The forest’s whisper no longer frightens; the blade feels familiar.”*
**Requirements:**

* 5 completed quests
* 10 confirmed monster kills (Tier: Normal)

**Privileges:**

* Access to Forest Tier I
* Can take escort, gathering, and minor hunting quests
* Can register for basic expeditions

---

## **D — Apprentice**

*“One who has tasted battle and did not falter.”*
**Requirements:**

* 20 completed quests
* 1 Elite monster kill
* Guild evaluation test

**Privileges:**

* Access to Forest Tier II
* Allowed to join mid-tier party hunts
* Eligible to train under veteran adventurers
* Access to improved equipment tiers

---

## **C — Adept**

*“Adventurer whose name is spoken in taverns with nods of respect.”*
**Requirements:**

* 40+ quests
* 3 Elite kills
* 1 Boss encounter (survival required, kill not necessary)

**Privileges:**

* Access to restricted territories
* Eligible for guild stipends
* Priority in mission assignment

---

## **B — Veteran**

*“A seasoned slayer who understands both triumph and loss.”*
**Requirements:**

* 80+ quests
* 1 confirmed Forest Boss kill
* Party leadership certification

**Privileges:**

* Command rights in guild missions
* Access to rare equipment & contracts
* Eligible for guild investigative missions
* Discounts at approved forges and inns

---

## **A — Master Adventurer**

*“A blade or spell feared by beasts and revered by the guild.”*
**Requirements:**

* 150+ quests
* 5 Boss kills
* Flawless evaluation trial

**Privileges:**

* Access to high-risk zones
* Eligible for solo S-rank mission trials
* Priority support from guild medics, mages, and scouts
* Can sponsor lower-rank adventurers

---

## **S — Elite**

*“One who walks the thin line between myth and mortality.”*
**Requirements:**

* 300 quests
* 10 Boss kills
* Completion of one “Calamity-class” threat assignment

**Privileges:**

* Access to classified missions
* Guild special funds access
* Authority to negotiate with regional lords on guild behalf
* Personalized rank insignia

---

## **SS — Paragon**

*“A legend whose presence bends fate itself.”*
**Requirements:**

* 500 quests
* 3 region-changing events survived
* Guild council vote

**Privileges:**

* Access to all guild secrets
* Command of guild battalions
* Rights to form an Adventurer Order
* Lifetime pension

---

## **SSS — Mythic**

*“A name etched into history. A slayer of calamities. A breaker of destinies.”*
**Requirements:**

* Achievements that defy mortal measure
* Fame across nations
* Deeds that alter the course of the world

**Privileges:**

* No quest restrictions
* Titles bestowed by kingdoms
* Eternal guild enshrinement
* Access to the Forbidden Library
* Personalized Mythic-grade artifact (story-based unlock)
