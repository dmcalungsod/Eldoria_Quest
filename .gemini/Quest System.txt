# ⭐ **1. Introduction**

The world of **Eldoria** has fractured since the Veil cracked, releasing shadows, spirits, and corrupted beasts into once-peaceful regions. The **Adventurer’s Guild** stands as the last bulwark between order and chaos—an institution where wanderers, mages, sellswords, and fledgling heroes gather to earn coin, hone skill, and gradually stake their claim in legend.

Quests in Eldoria are not mere chores—they are **living stories**, threads woven into the great tapestry of a world struggling to heal.

As a newly registered adventurer, your journey begins now.

# ⭐ **2. How the Quest System Works**

### **Step 1 — Join the Adventurer’s Guild**

After character creation, the guild registrar invites you:

> *“Before you wield steel or spell against the darkness, you must take the Oath.
> Step forward and claim your place among Eldoria’s defenders.”*

The player uses:

```
!guild register
```

You are assigned the starting rank: **F-Rank Initiate**.

# ⭐ **5. Accepting a Quest**

Players take a quest with:

```
!quest accept <quest_id>
```

Bot reply:

> *“The parchment is yours. The Guild seal burns briefly across its top—marking your oath to see the task through.”*

The quest is now tracked under:

```
!myquests
```

---

# ⭐ **6. Progress Tracking**

Progress automatically updates when the player performs relevant actions:

Examples:

* Killing **Forest Slime** increments progress on Quest 001.
* Gathering **Moonpetal Herb** increments progress on Gathering Quests.
* Entering a marked location affects Investigation Quests.

Players can check their status:

```
!quest progress <quest_id>
```

Bot example:

> **Progress for Quest 001**
> Forest Slimes defeated: **3 / 5**
> *The creek still trembles with gelatinous shapes. Your work is not yet done.*

---

# ⭐ **7. Completing a Quest**

When requirements are met:

```
!quest complete <quest_id>
```

Bot:

> *“With task fulfilled, you return to the Guild Hall. The guild clerk inspects your report, nods, and presses a wax seal upon it—a mark of completion. Your reward is counted with care.”*

Rewards automatically applied to:

* EXP
* Gold
* Items inventory
* Guild Reputation (optional)

If EXP thresholds are met, the bot triggers:

> **You have leveled up!**
> *A quiet strength settles into your limbs as your experience grows.*

# ⭐ **8. Quest Tiers & Guild Ranks**

| Guild Rank            | Tier   | Difficulty | Unlocks                                     |
| --------------------- | ------ | ---------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **F-Rank Initiate**   | F-Tier | Very Easy  | Basic monster hunts, simple gathering       |
| **E-Rank Novice**     | E-Tier | Easy       | Goblin patrols, spider nests, escort quests |
| **D-Rank Apprentice** | D-Tier | Moderate   | Boss hunts, corrupted forest sites          |

Promotion happens when completing required quests:

* **10 F-Tier quests → E-Rank**
* **12 E-Tier quests → D-Rank**

Bot example:

> *“Your deeds echo across the Guild Hall.
> By order of the Silvercrest Guildmaster, you are hereby elevated to **E-Rank Adventurer**.”*
