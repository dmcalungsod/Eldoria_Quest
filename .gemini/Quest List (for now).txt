Generate **all 30 Beginner-Area Forest Quests** in **Format A**: compact, clean, lore-rich, and ready for direct use in your Discord bot.

Each quest includes:

* **Quest ID**
* **Quest Tier**
* **Quest Giver**
* **Location**
* **Summary**
* **Description (1–2 paragraphs)**
* **Objectives**
* **Rewards**

*These are balanced for Rank F → Rank D progression.*
