# Region Design: The Undergrove

## Core Identity
- **Name:** The Undergrove
- **Theme:** A massive, subterranean jungle illuminated by eerie bioluminescence and toxic fungal spores. It is both beautiful and deeply hazardous, overgrown with alien-looking flora.
- **History:** Following The Sundering, massive fissures swallowed entire ancient forests. Over decades, cut off from sunlight and exposed to raw, subterranean mana vents, the flora mutated. It is now a closed ecosystem of hyper-aggressive plant and fungal life.
- **Culture:** The region is largely uninhabited by civilized folk, save for daring Alchemists and specialized foragers who risk their lives for the incredibly rare reagents found within.

## Geography & Locations
- **The Spore Falls:** A cascading waterfall of glowing, toxic pollen that serves as the primary entrance to the deeper sections.
- **The Rotting Bole:** A massive, hollowed-out petrified tree that serves as a makeshift, temporary sanctuary for foragers.
- **The Mycelial Network:** A sprawling, maze-like area where the ground itself is alive and reacts to movement.

## Inhabitants
- **Factions:** The Spore-Gatherers (a loosely organized group of black-market alchemists and reckless foragers).
- **Notable NPCs:**
  - **Morwen the Tainted:** A brilliant but unhinged alchemist who has survived in the Undergrove by micro-dosing its toxins.
- **Monster Ecology:** Fungal Hulks (reanimated beasts controlled by cordyceps-like fungi), Spore-Stalkers, Bioluminescent Myriapods.

## Gameplay Elements
- **Access Requirements:** Rank B and completion of the quest "Into the Bioluminescence".
- **Exploration Mechanics:** Toxin accumulation. Players continuously build up a 'Toxin' meter while exploring. If it caps, they suffer severe stat penalties and health drain. Requires 'Purifying Brews' or 'Respirator Masks' to survive long expeditions.
- **Quests & Storylines:**
  - "The Tainted Cure": Harvest rare Lunawort and Primordial Ooze for Astraeon's infirmary and the new Alchemist guild.
- **Resources & Economy:** Primordial Ooze, Lunawort, Fungal Spores, Bioluminescent Sap.
- **Dangers & Challenges:** Environmental toxicity, blinding spore clouds (reduces accuracy), and enemies that explode into toxic clouds upon death.

## Integration with Existing Systems
- **DepthsWarden:** Potential to connect to deeper, Void-touched fungal caverns.
- **GameForge:** Needs to implement the Fungal Hulks, Spore-Stalkers, and the new alchemical materials (Lunawort, Primordial Ooze).
- **StoryWeaver:** Craft atmospheric descriptions of the glowing, suffocating environment.
- **Namewright:** Provide names for the specific flora and specialized alchemist tools.
- **Grimwarden:** Balance the 'Toxin' accumulation mechanic to ensure it's a constant, meaningful threat.
- **GameBalancer:** Evaluate the Expected Value (EV) of the rare alchemical reagents to not break the economy.
- **ProgressionBalancer:** Validate the Rank B requirement.
- **Tactician:** Design combat mechanics for monsters that explode on death or inflict stacking poisons.
- **ChronicleKeeper:** Add achievements like "Spore Survivor" or "Master Gatherer".
- **Equipper:** Design Respirator Masks and Purifying Brews needed for exploration.
