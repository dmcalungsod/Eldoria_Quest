# Region Design: The Silent City of Ouros

## Core Identity
- **Name:** The Silent City of Ouros
- **Theme:** Eerie, dead metropolis. perfectly preserved but completely devoid of life, except for the lingering echoes of The Sundering.
- **History:** Once a jewel of ancient human civilization, Ouros was swallowed whole by the earth during the immediate aftermath of The Sundering. The temporal anomalies perfectly preserved its architecture.
- **Culture:** The ancient Ourosans were obsessed with temporal magic and clockwork. Their statues still stand, their streets still clean, yet the silence is absolute and maddening.

## Geography
- **The Grand Promenade** – A massive central avenue lined with petrified trees and silent, motionless fountains.
- **The Chronomancer's Spire** – A towering structure in the city center where temporal anomalies are strongest.
- **The Sunken Districts** – Areas where the earth hasn't perfectly preserved the city, showing signs of crushing pressure and abyssal decay.

## Inhabitants
- **Factions:** The Echoes (remnants of the past trapped in time loops), The Void Seekers (explorers driven mad by the silence).
- **Monsters:** Temporal Wraiths, Hollowed Sentinels, Abyssal Creepers, Echoing Behemoth (rare).

## Gameplay
- **Access:** Rank S or completion of "The Whispering Archives" mystery quest. Level 42 requirement.
- **Mechanics:** Absolute silence mechanic (sound-based skills fail, stealth is mandatory to avoid temporal anomalies).
- **Resources:** Chronal Dust, Perfected Glass, Ancient Ourosan Coin, Void Touched Relic.
- **Dangers:** Temporal rifts that instantly age or revert characters, absolute sensory deprivation causing madness/fatigue.

## Agent Coordination
- **DepthsWarden** – Needs to connect Ouros to the deep subterranean layers beneath the Void Sanctum.
- **GameForge** – Needs to create the Temporal Wraiths and Hollowed Sentinels, and the resources like Chronal Dust.
- **StoryWeaver** – The flavor text must heavily emphasize the oppressive silence and the unnerving perfection of the city.
- **Namewright** – May need to name specific NPCs or statues found within the city.
- **Grimwarden** – Ensure the madness/fatigue mechanics from sensory deprivation are implemented for long expeditions.
- **GameBalancer** – Balance the high rewards of ancient relics with the extreme danger of temporal anomalies.
- **ProgressionBalancer** – Ensure Rank S is appropriate for the level 42 requirement.
- **Tactician** – Create the "silence" mechanic where certain skills cannot be used.
- **ChronicleKeeper** – Add an achievement for exploring the Chronomancer's Spire.
- **EventHerald** – Could create a "Time Quake" event where the city briefly animates.
- **Lorekeeper** – Ensure Ouros' history fits with the established lore of The Sundering's temporal effects.
- **Equipper** – New gear sets crafted from Perfected Glass and Chronal Dust.
- **Palette** – Add UI indicators for the "Absolute Silence" status effect.
- **BugHunter** – Test the new silence mechanics and temporal rift events in the auto-adventure loop.
