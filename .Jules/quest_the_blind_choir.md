# Quest Design: The Blind Choir's Requiem

📜 **Quest:** The Blind Choir's Requiem

🎭 **Giver:** "Whispering" Thorne (Paranoid survivor mapping the chasm with sound)

📖 **Story:**
The Deep Delvers in The Wailing Chasm have adapted to the dark, but some have gone completely mad, forming "The Blind Choir". Led by the Choirmaster, they sing a forbidden requiem to the void below. This harmonic resonance is beginning to widen the chasm, threatening to swallow the entire region. The player must descend, disrupt their ritual, and ultimately decide the fate of their unholy text.

🗺️ **Objectives:**
- **The Blind Choir's Requiem: Resonance:**
  - `destroy: Resonance Crystal (3)` (Disrupting the harmonic resonance of the ritual)
  - `retrieve: Hymnal of the Void` (The source of their corrupting song)
- **The Blind Choir's Requiem: Silence (Choice A):**
  - `defeat: Choirmaster (1), Blind Choir Zealot (2)`
  - `use: Sonic-Dampening Bomb` (Silencing the Choirmaster permanently)
- **The Blind Choir's Requiem: Discord (Choice B):**
  - `defeat: Choirmaster (1), Blind Choir Zealot (4)` (Harder fight as the Choirmaster uses the void's power)

⚖️ **Choices:**
- **Choice A (Silence):** Destroy the Hymnal and use a Sonic-Dampening Bomb. (Rewards an Echolocation Helm recipe, title "The Silencer").
- **Choice B (Discord):** Keep the Hymnal for its dark secrets. Thorne abandons the player, making the final fight harder. (Rewards the "Hymnal of the Void" item, title "Discordant Hero").

🏆 **Rewards:**
- Resonance: 4000 EXP, 1000 Aurum
- Silence: 5500 EXP, 1800 Aurum, Echolocation Helm Recipe, Title "The Silencer"
- Discord: 6500 EXP, 2000 Aurum, Hymnal of the Void, Title "Discordant Hero"
