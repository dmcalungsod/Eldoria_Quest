# Region Design: The Celestial Archipelago

## Core Identity
- **Name:** The Celestial Archipelago
- **Theme:** Floating Ruins, Ancient Magic, Failing Gravity.
- **History:** Once the city of *Aethoria*, raised by Arch-Mages to escape the Sundering. The magic is now failing, leaving the ruins drifting in the upper atmosphere.
- **Cultural Flavor:** Ancient runes, star-metal architecture, constructs powered by celestial energy.

## Geography
- **Drifting Isles:** Platforms of stone and metal floating on mana currents.
- **The Central Spire:** Where the Celestial Arbiter resides, guarding the city's heart.
- **Hazards:** High winds, vertigo, unstable magic.

## Inhabitants
- **Monsters:**
    - **Arcane Sentinel (Lvl 28):** Stone guardians.
    - **Aether Wisp (Lvl 28):** Unstable mana motes.
    - **Sky-Ray (Lvl 29):** Aerial predators.
    - **Ruin Stalker (Lvl 29 Elite):** Wraiths of the old inhabitants.
    - **The Celestial Arbiter (Lvl 31 Boss):** A massive construct.

## Gameplay
- **Access:** Rank B (Level 28+).
- **Resources:**
    - **Aether Stone:** Common magic conductor.
    - **Star Metal Fragment:** Uncommon crafting material.
    - **Ancient Tablet:** Rare lore item.
    - **Celestial Core:** Epic boss drop.
- **Dangers:** Gravity manipulation attacks, high damage elemental beams.

## Integration
- Fills the progression gap between Frostfall Expanse (Lvl 25) and Molten Caldera (Lvl 30).
- Introduces "Star Metal" and "Celestial" themed items for future crafting recipes.
