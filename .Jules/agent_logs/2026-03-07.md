## ⚖️ ProgressionBalancer
**Focus:** Rank D Tuning & Endgame Boss Smoothing

**Completed:**
- **Rank D Adjustment:** Increased `normal_kills` requirement from 250 to **300**. This enforces a distinct gap between quest completion (which yields ~60-100 kills) and rank advancement, ensuring players engage in non-quest exploration or auto-adventures.
- **Endgame Tuning:** Reduced Boss Kill requirements for Rank A (8->6), S (15->10), and SS (25->20). Given the 5% boss spawn rate, the previous numbers created an excessive "luck wall" rather than a skill challenge.
- **Verification:** Added `tests/test_progression_balance.py` to verify `RankSystem` respects the new thresholds.
