# 📜 Namewright Log - 2026-02-25

## 🌩️ Gale-Scarred Heights (Location Package)
**Context:** Filling the Level 30-40 gap (Bridge between Molten Caldera and Void Sanctum).
**Theme:** Storm/Sky, Verticality, Elemental Instability.
**For:** @GameForge, @DepthsWarden, @Foreman (Auto-Adventure Phase 2)

### 📍 Location Concept
**Name:** **Gale-Scarred Heights** (Level 35 | Rank A)
**Description:** A cluster of floating islands suspended in a perpetual storm. Lightning arcs constantly between the peaks, and the wind is strong enough to scour flesh from bone.
**Sub-Zones:**
1.  **The Cloud-Shear Cliffs** – The outer rim where wind blades are literal hazards.
2.  **Fulminating Spire** – A natural lightning rod of conductive stone.
3.  **The Eye of the Storm** – A deceptive calm at the center, hiding the boss.

### 🦅 Monsters (IDs 136-140)
*   **136: Razor-Wing Harpy (Common)** – Harpies with metallic feathers honed by the wind.
*   **137: Cloud-Stalker Leopard (Uncommon)** – A predator with fur that shifts color like clouds, ambushing from mist.
*   **138: Volt-Vulture (Rare)** – Scavengers that feed on lightning-struck corpses; they discharge static when hit.
*   **139: Fulminating Golem (Elite)** – A construct of storm-charged stone that explodes on death.
*   **140: Tempestus, The Storm King (Boss)** – An ancient storm elemental bound to a massive suit of Sky-Iron armor.

### ⚔️ Items & Materials
*   **Charged Feather** – Drop from Harpies; used for crafting arrows or light armor.
*   **Fulgurite Shard** – Lightning-fused glass; used for lenses or magic focus.
*   **Cloud-Cotton** – Soft, airy material harvested from sky-plants; used for stealth gear.
*   **Storm-Core** – Rare drop from Golems/Boss; pulses with infinite energy.
*   **Sky-Iron Ore** – Meteoritic iron found only on the highest peaks; extremely light but strong.

### 📜 Quest Hooks
*   **The Eye of the Storm** – Reach the center of the heights to retrieve a lost artifact.
*   **Lightning Harvest** – Collect Fulgurite Shards during a storm surge.
*   **Grounded** – Clip the wings of 20 Razor-Wing Harpies to make the skies safer.

### 📝 Notes
- **Tone:** High-energy, dangerous, exhilarating.
- **Mechanic Idea:** "Wind Shear" debuff that reduces accuracy or pushes players back.
- **Color Palette:** Grey, Electric Blue, White, Silver.
