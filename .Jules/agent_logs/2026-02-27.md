- Addressing the **"Critical Gap"** identified by Lorekeeper (2026-02-26): Lack of quests for the new Sky Realms locations.
- Developed a narrative progression ("The Skybound Arc") moving from the Ashlands up to the Gale-Scarred Heights.

### 📜 Generated Quest Names

#### Part 1: The Ashlands (Rank D, Lvl 12)
*Theme: Discovery, looking upward, finding the path.*
1.  **Shard of the Sky** – Investigating a fallen piece of a floating island.
2.  **Gaze of the Ash** – Finding a vantage point above the smog to see the archipelago.
3.  **The First Ascent** – Scaling a high peak in the Ashlands to reach a transport rift.

#### Part 2: The Celestial Archipelago (Rank B, Lvl 28)
*Theme: Disorientation, ancient magic, gravity.*
1.  **Echoes of Gravity** – Stabilizing a collapsing island ruin.
2.  **Void-Touched Winds** – Surviving a magical storm in the upper atmosphere.
3.  **The Silent Observatory** – Finding an ancient listening post.

#### Part 3: Gale-Scarred Heights (Rank A, Lvl 35)
*Theme: Survival, the summit, the ultimate test.*
1.  **Breath of the Storm** – Reaching the highest peak where the air is thinnest.
2.  **The Summit’s Price** – A test of endurance against the elements.
3.  **Wind-Walker’s Legacy** – Uncovering the fate of a legendary Pathfinder who disappeared here.
