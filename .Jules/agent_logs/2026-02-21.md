## 📜 Namewright Log - 2026-02-21

### ⚗️ The Alchemist Class (Naming Package)
**Context:** Grim survival, practical science over magic, "using the dirt."
**For:** @Architect, @GameForge, @Tactician

#### 🧪 Skill Names (Flavor Upgrades)
*   **Acid Flask** → **Vitriol Phial** (Sounds more archaic/scientific), **Etching Draught**, **Corrosive Salvo**.
*   **Field Triage** → **Suture & Salve**, **Battle-Mend**, **Cauterize** (If it involves fire/pain).
*   **Volatile Brew** → **Fulminating Flask** (Fulminate = explode), **Brimstone Jar**, **Ignition Oil**.
*   **Adrenal Injector** → **Quicksilver Blood**, **Vitality Spike**, **Haste Serum**.
*   **Scavenger's Intuition** → **Vulture's Eye**, **Grave-Digger's Luck**, **Waste-Not**.

#### 🗡️ Weapons (Scalpels & Tools)
*   **Chirurgeon's Scalpel** – Precise, sterile, deadly.
*   **Bone-Saw Dagger** – Serrated for cutting through monster carapace.
*   **Injector Blade** – A dagger with a hollow channel for delivering toxins.
*   **Glass-Shard Shiv** – Improvised, desperate, sharp.
*   **The Blood-Letter** – A unique/rare scalpel.

#### 🏷️ Class Titles & Ranks
1.  **Scavenger** (Novice) – Gathering parts.
2.  **Mixologist** (Apprentice) – Brewing basics.
3.  **Apothecary** (Journeyman) – Healing and harming.
4.  **Chirurgeon** (Expert) – Master of anatomy.
5.  **Transmuter** (Master) – Changing the nature of things.

#### 📜 Quest Concepts
*   **The Theory of Decay** – Study how monsters rot to create better poisons.
*   **Blood for Blood** – Collect rare samples to cure a village plague.
*   **The Alchemist's Debt** – Repay a loan to a shady guild using monster parts.

#### 📝 Notes
*   Avoid "Potion" where possible; use "Draught", "Phial", "Serum", "Tincture" to sound more clinical/archaic.
*   The vibe should be "Mad Doctor" meets "Survivalist".
