## 2023-10-27 — Dynamic Time-Based Encounters

**Learning:** Static encounter tables break immersion by ignoring the passage of time. Implementing a simple real-time check (UTC hour) allows for nocturnal predators, making the world feel alive and dangerous at night without complex simulation systems.
**Action:** Future location designs should always consider "Day" and "Night" variants for monster pools to reinforce the survival horror aspect of darkness.

## 2024-05-23 — Dynamic Scarcity Pricing

**Learning:** Static shop prices with infinite stock trivialized resource management. Implementing a dynamic "hoarding tax" (price increases per item owned) simulates scarcity and demand without complex inventory tracking or hard limits. This reinforces the survival theme by discouraging stockpiling and making each potion feel precious.
**Action:** Consider applying similar dynamic scarcity mechanics to other resource sinks, like crafting or travel costs, to maintain economic tension throughout progression.
