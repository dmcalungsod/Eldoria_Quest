# Region Design: The Thunder-Crag Coast

## Core Identity
- **Name:** The Thunder-Crag Coast
- **Theme:** A treacherous shoreline of jagged iron-rich cliffs, battered by perpetual storms and littered with the wreckage of an ancient fleet.
- **History:** Centuries ago, the "Iron Armada" set sail to confront the encroaching Void but was annihilated by a supernatural tempest during the Sundering. The cliffs, rich in magnetic iron, now act as natural lightning rods, grounding the ceaseless storm.
- **Cultural Flavor:** A grim, salty frontier. "Wreck-Divers" brave the electrified waters to salvage ancient technology and precious metals from the rusted hulks.

## Geography & Locations
- **The Rusted Bay:** A cemetery of ships, half-buried in sand and salt.
- **Fulgurite Beach:** A stretch of sand fused into glass by constant lightning strikes.
- **The Howling Grotto:** A sea cave that channels the wind into a deafening scream, home to sirens.
- **Storm-Watch Bluff:** The highest point, constantly struck by lightning, where the boss resides.

## Inhabitants
- **Factions:**
    - **Wreck-Divers:** Scavengers seeking "Ancient Iron" and lost tech.
    - **The Drowned Guard:** Undead sailors still loyal to their admiral.
- **Monsters:**
    - **Storm-Shell Crab (Lvl 32):** Its shell conducts electricity.
    - **Drowned Marine (Lvl 33):** Wields rusted, waterlogged weapons.
    - **Volt-Eel (Lvl 32):** Lurks in rock pools, shocking unwary steppers.
    - **Siren Matriarch (Lvl 34 Elite):** Uses sonic attacks and water magic.
    - **Admiral Racker (Lvl 35 Boss):** The undead admiral of the Iron Armada, fused with the wreckage of his ship.

## Gameplay Elements
- **Access Requirements:** Rank A (Level 33).
- **Exploration Mechanics:** "Static Charge" mechanic - staying too long increases damage taken from lightning attacks. Rain reduces fire effectiveness but boosts lightning.
- **Resources:**
    - **Storm Essence:** For crafting lightning-imbued gear.
    - **Ancient Iron (Rusted Scrap):** High-quality metal for smithing.
    - **Siren Scale:** Alchemical ingredient.
    - **Fulgurite Shard:** Rare glass for optical/magical gear.
- **Dangers:**
    - **Lightning Strikes:** Random environmental damage.
    - **Electrified Water:** Standing in water applies "Shock".

## Integration with Existing Systems
- **Guild Ranks:** Bridges the gap between Molten Caldera (Lvl 30) and Gale-Scarred Heights (Lvl 35).
- **Crafting:** Introduces `Ancient Iron` (via Rusted Scrap) and `Storm Essence`.
- **Quests:** "Salvage the Logbook", "Put the Admiral to Rest".
