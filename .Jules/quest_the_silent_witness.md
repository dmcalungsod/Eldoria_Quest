## 🎭 Quest 1: The Missing Ledger

**ID:** 61
**Tier:** F
**Giver:** Clerk Elara (Willowcreek Outskirts)
**Prerequisite:** None

### 📖 Narrative Hook
Elara is frantic. While fleeing from a slime ambush near the forest edge, she dropped a ledger containing sensitive Guild transaction records. If the Guildmaster finds out, she'll lose her position—or worse. She begs you to retrieve it before the slimes digest the evidence.

### 🗺️ Objectives
1.  **Locate:** Torn Page (Evidence of the ledger's fate).
2.  **Examine:** Slime Trails (To find where the creature went).
3.  **Defeat:** Verdant Slime (x5) (Eliminate the culprits).

### 🗣️ Dialogue Script

**Start (Elara):**
> "You there! Please, keep your voice down. I... I've made a terrible mistake. I was transporting the archive ledger—routine transfer!—when a massive slime lunged at me. I panicked. I ran. The ledger... it's gone. If the Guildmaster finds out, I'm finished. Please, retrace my steps near the outskirts. It has to be there!"

**Completion (Elara):**
> "You found a page? Oh, thank the stars... wait. This isn't just slime damage. These marks... someone *tore* this out before the slime got it. And this ink... it's fresh. Someone was reading it. Thank you for this, adventurer. I need to take this to Scout Valen immediately."

### ⚖️ Choices & Consequences
*   **Choice:** Keep the secret or Report to Guildmaster? (Implied in next quest interaction).
*   **Consequence:** Unlocks Quest 2.

## 🎭 Quest 2: The Shadowed Trail

**ID:** 62
**Tier:** E
**Giver:** Scout Valen (Whispering Thicket)
**Prerequisite:** The Missing Ledger (Quest 61)

### 📖 Narrative Hook
Elara brought the torn page to Valen. He confirmed the markings are a code used by goblin scouts, but the code itself is too complex for standard goblins. Someone is feeding them intel. He needs you to investigate a suspected goblin camp in the Whispering Thicket where they might be decoding the rest of the ledger.

### 🗺️ Objectives
1.  **Examine:** Abandoned Camp (Look for clues of the mastermind).
2.  **Gather:** Moonleaf Herb (x3) (Valen needs these to reveal invisible ink on a map found at the camp).
3.  **Defeat:** Goblin Scout (x5) (Clear the spies).

### 🗣️ Dialogue Script

**Start (Valen):**
> "Elara told me what happened. That wasn't just a clumsy accident. The goblins were *waiting* for her. They wanted that ledger. I've tracked a group of scouts to the Whispering Thicket. They're moving with unusual purpose. Go there. Find their camp. And bring me anything that looks like orders."

**Completion (Valen):**
> "Moonleaf? Good thinking. Let's see what this map hides... *He applies the herb juice.* detailed patrol routes. Supply schedules. This isn't just goblin mischief. This is a siege plan. And look here—a signature. 'The Merchant'. We need to take this to Captain Rhea. Now."

### ⚖️ Choices & Consequences
*   **Consequence:** Unlocks Quest 3.

---

## 🎭 Quest 3: The Truth Revealed

**ID:** 63
**Tier:** E
**Giver:** Captain Rhea (Whispering Thicket)
**Prerequisite:** The Shadowed Trail (Quest 62)

### 📖 Narrative Hook
Captain Rhea has mobilized a strike team based on your findings. The "Merchant" has hired a mercenary band to coordinate the goblins, using the stolen ledger to bypass Guild patrols. The leader of this band, a Bramble Goblin lieutenant known as "Needle-Tooth", is holding the rest of the ledger as leverage.

### 🗺️ Objectives
1.  **Defeat:** Bramble Goblin (x1) (Defeat the lieutenant).
2.  **Retrieve:** Decoded Ledger (Secure the proof).

### 🗣️ Dialogue Script

**Start (Rhea):**
> "Valen showed me the map. We have a traitor in our midst, funding these attacks to drive up protection prices. But first, we cut off the hand that holds the weapon. The goblin lieutenant, Needle-Tooth, is hiding deep in the thicket. He has the ledger. Retrieve it, and we can end this."

**Completion (Rhea):**
> "It's done. With this ledger, we can trace the payments back to the source. You've done more than just kill monsters today, adventurer. You've saved the Guild's integrity. Walk tall. The Pathfinders will hear of this."

### 🏆 Rewards
*   **Title:** "Silent Witness"
*   **Item:** Guild Seal (Rare Material)
*   **Reputation:** +50 Guild
